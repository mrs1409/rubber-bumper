About Rubber Bumper

Rubber Bumper Co. is a small family-owned producer of rubber products with a focus on quality. The company manufactures two main products: rubber bands and condoms, and is a market leader in both categories.

Despite stable sales over the last 3 years, the company has been experiencing decreasing profits. This case study explores the strategic options available to the company, including the potential conversion of their rubber band factory.

Our chatbot assistant is designed to help you analyze this business case by providing instant access to relevant information about the company's products, market position, financial data, and strategic options.
